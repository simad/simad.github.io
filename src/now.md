I am now in Malta 🏝️

Some lessons I've been pondering, in random order:

- Always pick the choice that expands your life. It might not end up being the "right choice" (what does that even mean?) but it will satisfy you deeply.
- Living in a place where tomatoes actually have flavour and the fruit is delicious is very underrated.
- If you have to judge yourself on any metric, why not pick how fun your life is, how kind you are to people, and what marks you are leaving on this world?
- I do feel at least 20% more myself when I have a tan.

AI has not taken my job yet, but I am nevertheless ready for my full-time farmer/pirate/scientist era.
